Are certain genes bound or amplified more?


The amplification bias discovered on the previous page may be due to the gene sequence of the transcript rather than the UMI. A potential gene bias is two-fold: UMIs might preferentially bind certain genes and certain genes might themselves be amplified more often than others. The genes used in this part of the study are from a set of 1,011 genes that infrequently have zero expression in a cell.
